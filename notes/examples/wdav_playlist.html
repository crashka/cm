7:00 AM Biscuits and Bach
Johann Sebastian Bach’s Partitas, BWV 825-830, were published between 1726 and 1730, and were the first of his works to be published under his direction. They were among the last of his keyboard suites to be composed, and are by far the most technically demanding. Massachusetts based, Russian born American pianist, Sergey Schepkin, is a leading authority on the interpretation of Bach’s work. This week we’ll listen to his brand new release on the Steinway and Sons label called “Bach: The Six Partitas.”
